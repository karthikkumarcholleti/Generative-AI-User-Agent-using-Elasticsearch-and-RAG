## Category Breakdown

### 1. Vital Signs
- Heart rate / Pulse
- Blood pressure (systolic, diastolic, **mean** - NEW)
- Body temperature
- Respiratory rate
- Oxygen saturation
- Body height, weight, BMI

### 2. Basic Metabolic Panel ⭐ RENAMED
- Glucose / Blood sugar
- Creatinine
- Protein, Albumin
- **Electrolytes:** Sodium, Potassium, Chloride, Carbon dioxide
- Magnesium, Calcium
- Urea nitrogen (BUN)
- **Anion gap** (NEW)
- **Lactate** (NEW)

### 3. Liver Function Tests
- ALT (Alanine Aminotransferase)
- AST (Aspartate Aminotransferase)
- Alkaline Phosphatase
- **Bilirubin** (total, non-glucuronidated) - MOVED FROM LAB
- Albumin/Globulin Ratio

### 4. Lipid Panel ⭐ NEW CATEGORY
- **Cholesterol** (total, HDL, LDL) - MOVED FROM LAB
- **Triglycerides**
- **Cholesterol ratios**

### 5. Complete Blood Count - Red Blood Cells ⭐ SPLIT FROM BLOOD COUNT
- Red blood cells (RBC / Erythrocytes)
- **Hemoglobin** (MOVED FROM LAB)
- Hematocrit
- MCV, MCH, MCHC
- **Erythrocyte Distribution Width (RDW)** (NEW)

### 6. Complete Blood Count - White Blood Cells ⭐ SPLIT FROM BLOOD COUNT
- White blood cells (WBC / Leukocytes)
- Neutrophils (absolute and %)
- Lymphocytes (absolute and %)
- Monocytes (absolute and %)
- Eosinophils (absolute and %)
- Basophils (absolute and %)

### 7. Complete Blood Count - Platelets ⭐ SPLIT FROM BLOOD COUNT
- Platelets
- **Platelet Mean Volume** (NEW)

### 8. Hemoglobin & Diabetes Monitoring ⭐ NEW CATEGORY
- Hemoglobin (Hgb)
- **Hemoglobin A1C (HbA1c)** (NEW)
- **HbA1c/Hemoglobin ratio** (NEW)

### 9. Kidney Function ⭐ NEW CATEGORY
- **GFR** (MOVED FROM LAB)
- **Urea Nitrogen/Creatinine ratio**

### 10. Cardiac Markers ⭐ NEW CATEGORY
- **Troponin I (cardiac)** (NEW)
- Use case: Heart attack detection

### 11. Hormones ⭐ NEW CATEGORY
- **TSH (Thyroid Stimulating Hormone)** (NEW)

### 12. Coagulation Studies ⭐ NEW CATEGORY
- **INR (International Normalized Ratio)** (NEW)
- **Coagulation times** (NEW)
- **Heparin** (NEW)

### 13. Therapeutic Drug Monitoring ⭐ NEW CATEGORY
- **Vancomycin levels** (peak) (NEW)
- Heparin levels

### 14. Enzymes & Metabolic Tests ⭐ NEW CATEGORY
- **Triacylglycerol lipase** (NEW)
- Lactate

### 15. Urine Analysis
- Urine pH
- Specific gravity
- Urobilinogen
- **Erythrocytes in urine** (NEW)
- **Leukocytes in urine** (NEW)

### 16. Behavioral Assessments
- Smoking history (pack-years, cigarettes/day)
- PHQ-9 Depression score
- Social activity questions

### 17. Other Observations
- Any observations not fitting the above categories
